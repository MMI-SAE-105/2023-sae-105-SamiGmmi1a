Art Brut exhibition

Exhibition dates: April 13 to June 23, 2023

Place of the exhibition: Fondation Cartier, 261 Bd Raspail, 75014 Paris

Opening hours: Every day from 11 a.m. to 8 p.m., except Monday. Nocturne on Tuesday, until 10 p.m. The closing of the rooms begins at 7:45 p.m. (9:45 p.m. on Tuesdays).

It was in 1945, after a trip to Switzerland, that Jean Dubuffet used this thermal bath for the first time. It designates the works produced by marginalized or interned asylum

The Art Brut works are made by self-taught creators, marginalized entrenched in a position of rebellious spirit or impervious to collective norms and values. They design a universe for their own use without worrying about public criticism or the gaze of others, recognition or any kind of approval.
